## Cross-Border Payment Economics

### Traditional vs Stablecoin

| Cost Component | Traditional | Via Stablecoins | Savings |
|----------------|-------------|-----------------|---------|
| FX Spread | 2-4% | 0.1-0.5% | 80-95% |
| Settlement Fee | 1-2% | 0.1-0.3% | 85-90% |
| Float Cost | 0.5-1% | 0% | 100% |
| Compliance | 0.5-1% | Included | Variable |
| **Total** | **5-7%** | **0.35-1.3%** | **~80%** |

### Bitso Business Economics (Brazil↔Mexico)

From Bitso's published data:
- BRL → USDC → MXN spreads: Under 10 basis points
- Legacy SWIFT spreads: 40-65 basis points
- Settlement time: 7-10 minutes (vs 48-hour SWIFT average)
