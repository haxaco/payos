# Potential Customers and Partners

**Last Updated:** January 2026  
**Source:** Market research, competitive analysis, PRD v1.16

---

## Executive Summary

PayOS targets three customer segments: (1) LATAM growth-stage fintechs needing agentic payment infrastructure, (2) US/Global remittance companies needing LATAM settlement rails, and (3) Procurement AI startups needing settlement to complete their workflows. This document catalogs potential customers discovered through competitive research.

---
