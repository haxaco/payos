# Scanner Partner Outreach Template

Short email to send when a potential design partner has been identified. Customize the **bold** sections; keep everything else as-is for consistency.

## Subject lines (pick one)

- `Agentic-commerce readiness data for {{Partner's product}}`
- `Prospect list: which merchants need {{Partner's product}} most`
- `Scanner data for the {{Partner domain}} team`

## Body

---

## Tips for the sender

- **Keep it specific.** The template's strongest line is the "{{one-line reference to Partner's product}}" — if you can't fill it in with something genuine (a tweet, a job post, a recent blog), don't send.
- **Lead with the prospect-qualification angle** for ecommerce-agent vendors. Lead with the synthetic-test report for payments/fintech. Lead with the corpus/coverage story for research and analyst prospects.
- **Attach the sample report as a PDF** (available at `scanner-reports/baseline-q1-2026-report.pdf`) if the recipient is slow to click links. Adds legitimacy and gets past "is this real?" hurdle.
- **CC `@founders-team`** on the first email so they can weigh in before the call.
- **Log the outreach** in the internal CRM / shared sheet so we don't double-send to the same prospect.
