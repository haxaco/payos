## Methodology

- **Scanner version:** v2 with status/confidence/eligibility detection
- **Protocol probes:** UCP, ACP, x402 (incl. Bazaar registry), AP2, MCP, NLWeb, Visa VIC, Mastercard AgentPay
- **Analyzers:** Structured data (JSON-LD, microdata, OpenGraph), accessibility (robots.txt, CAPTCHA, checkout), business model classification
- **Scoring:** Weighted composite of protocol (30%), data quality (25%), accessibility (20%), checkout (25%)
- **Scan date:** February 18, 2026
- **Batch:** UK-EU-LATAM-Africa 300 (batch_id: b6f17a41-a3f0-4288-bd3f-b3d042e8b0b4)
- **Runtime:** ~2 minutes for 300 domains at concurrency 10
- **Failure rate:** 0/300 (0%)
