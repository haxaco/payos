## Paging

- **Sev 1** — Production down or money at risk. On-call pages immediately.
- **Sev 2** — Degraded; some flows failing. On-call notified, response within 2h.
- **Sev 3** — Non-blocking bug with workaround.
- **Sev 4** — Feature request / question.

Defined in detail at [support SLAs](https://docs.getsly.ai/resources/support).

## Status page

Public status page at `status.getsly.ai`. Incident updates flow from our incident tooling to the status page automatically. If you're on-call, posting to the status page is part of response — partners depend on it.

## Post-incident

Every Sev 1 and Sev 2 gets:

- Timeline document (who did what when)
- Blameless retrospective within 5 business days
- Public postmortem within 7 business days (Sev 1 and 2 only)
- Runbook update if the incident revealed a gap

Postmortems live at `status.getsly.ai/history` (public-facing summaries) and in `docs/incidents/` internally (full timelines).

## When a runbook is missing or wrong

Open a PR. Runbooks are the most-used internal docs; quality matters more than finding it aesthetic. Update the step that tripped you up, add the command you wished was there, remove the step that no longer applies.
