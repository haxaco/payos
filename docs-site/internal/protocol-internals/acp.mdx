## File

## External spec

Stripe + OpenAI maintain the canonical spec. Our implementation must stay compliant with their updates — treat the spec as frozen unless they publish a new version.

## Sly-specific extensions

Beyond the canonical spec:
- KYA tier enforcement at `POST /v1/acp/checkouts/:id/complete`
- Wallet policy evaluation
- Composition with other protocols (UCP-funded ACP checkouts, etc.)
- Approval workflow hooks

Extensions use `x-` prefixed fields; never modify spec-defined fields.

## Connection to Stripe

Via `@stripe/*` official SDK. Credentials per-tenant in `tenants.stripe_connect_account_id`.

## Gotchas

- **Stripe webhooks vs. our webhooks** — we receive from Stripe at `/webhooks/stripe`, then re-emit our own events. Don't forward Stripe webhooks directly to partners.
- **Checkout session expiry** — Stripe defaults 24h; we mirror in our records but use our own TTL field for faster expiry in some configurations.
- **Batch checkouts** — Sly-specific batching over the Stripe session API. Each child is a real Stripe session; we just group them.
