## How to get help right now

- **Integration questions** — email `hello@getsly.ai`
- **Security disclosures** — email `security@getsly.ai`
- **Compliance / legal** — email `compliance@getsly.ai`
- **Privacy / data** — email `privacy@getsly.ai`
- **Dashboard Intercom chat** — in-product, fastest for account-specific issues
- **Your named CSM** — if you're on an enterprise plan

## Dashboard

For product-side tools that aren't yet public URLs, the [Sly dashboard](https://app.getsly.ai) exposes:

- Full operational status for your tenant (Settings → Status)
- Support chat (bottom-right in the dashboard)
- Change history for your tenant's activity
- Compliance document uploads (Settings → Compliance)

Public launches of these surfaces will be announced in the [changelog](/resources/changelog) and via email to the address on your tenant account.
